RAGECOOP

Drive around the interstate with your buddy, enjoy GTAs environment, make own missions and events or just chill in Grove Street!

Brief introduction

"Ever dreamed of using co-op mode in GTA5 story mode? Now it's a reality! Complete missions with your friends, experience random heisting, or just take a ride on the interstate together. ”


Installation:
1. Download the client.
2. Download and install ScriptHookVDotNet if you don't have it.
3. Drag and drop everything from the "RageCoop" folder in the zip to the Scripts/ folder located in the GTA5 root folder. If it doesn't exist, create one.
4. Once in the game, press F9 to open the menu and connect to the server.
